My Philosophy

“Librarian is a service occupation. Gas station attendant of the mind.” -Richard Powers

As an information professional there is no higher honor than the free provision of resources and services that allow people to think, speak, read, write, and act for themselves. Where the youngest members of society are concerned, it is not only an honor, but our collective obligation to plant the reading seeds early and make sure they take root, so the next generation can take advantage of all that the library, and the world, have to offer.
